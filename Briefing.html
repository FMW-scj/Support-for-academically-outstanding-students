A New Pilot Program for Our Top Achievers

Welcome to a new initiative designed to provide you with personalized, proactive support. This mentorship program connects a select group of students with dedicated teachers to help you navigate challenges, maximize your potential, and prepare for future success.

Investing in Your Potential and Well-being

This scheme recognizes that high achievement comes with unique pressures. Our goal is to provide a support system that addresses these challenges head-on while helping you grow beyond the curriculum.

🎯

Proactive Well-being

Get support to manage academic pressure and high expectations, preventing burnout and promoting a healthy balance.

🌱

Maximize Growth

Explore advanced topics and new challenges with guidance from a mentor, pushing your learning potential.

🚀

Future Ready

Receive personalized advice on leadership, university applications, and career paths to build a strong foundation for your future.

Who are the Mentees?

The program's first cohort is composed of 10 students who demonstrated outstanding academic performance. The group is evenly split between two year groups.

  • Top 5 Students from the 2024-2025 Form 1 cohort (now in F2).
  • Top 5 Students from the 2024-2025 Form 2 cohort (now in F3).

How the Program Works

The mentorship journey is structured around four key check-ins throughout the academic year. This timeline provides a consistent, flexible framework for you and your mentor to connect.

Your Mentor's Role

Your Supporting Teacher is more than just an instructor; they are your confidential guide and advocate within the school. They are here to listen, advise, and support you in a variety of ways.

🤝

Reliable Point of Contact

A trusted adult you can approach to discuss any issue, academic or personal, in a confidential setting.

🗓️

Flexible Check-ins

Your mentor will arrange meetings, but the format and timing are flexible to suit your needs and schedule.

💡

Personalized Guidance

Offering support tailored to your unique situation, helping you find resources and overcome challenges.
